Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 table spoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling:cheese,diced vegatables,cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. YOu can add a tablespoon of water oro milk for a fluffier texture
  2. Heat the pan:Place a nan-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
  3. Coook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add filling(optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen filling over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve:As the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge an dfold it over the filling.Let it cook for another minutes,hten slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot,withh additional salt and peppper if needed.
